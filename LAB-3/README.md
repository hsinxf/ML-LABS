# Titanic Dataset EDA

1. Overview
   - Dataset contains 891 rows and 12 columns.
2. Key Insights:
   - High survival rates for first-class passengers (+60%).
   - Most passengers were between 20-40 years old.
   - There is a positive relationship between fare and survival
   - Female passengers has significantly higher survival rates than males.

3. Visual Insights:
   - Survival Rate by Passenger Class.
   - Survival Rate By Gender.
   - Age Distribution.
   - Age vs Fare Relationship.
